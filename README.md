## 🔥 Project Overview

Christ Revolution Ministries is redesigning its website to elevate its digital presence, foster deeper engagement, and expand outreach through interactive features and e-commerce capabilities. The new platform will serve as a dynamic hub for worship, learning, giving, and community connection.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Deliver a visually compelling, spiritually resonant homepage  
- Enhance user experience through intuitive navigation and mobile responsiveness  
- Introduce interactive features for deeper engagement  
- Launch a secure, user-friendly church store for merchandise and resources  
- Integrate multimedia, online giving, and event registration  
- Ensure scalability and maintainability for future growth

---

## 🧭 Scope of Work

### 1. Discovery & Planning
- Audit existing content and structure  
- Define user personas and digital journeys  
- Benchmark top-performing ministry websites  
- Align design with ministry branding and values

### 2. Design Strategy
- Three homepage layout variations for comparison  
- Integration of logo, color palette, and typography  
- Visual hierarchy and mobile-first design  
- Accessibility and readability enhancements

### 3. Interactive Features
- Live Chat or Prayer Request Widget  
- Interactive Sermon Library with filters  
- Event RSVP System with calendar sync  
- Newsletter Signup with segmented mailing lists  
- Testimonial Carousel  
- Social Media Feed Integration

### 4. E-Commerce Capabilities
- Church Store for merchandise and spiritual resources  
- Secure checkout (Stripe, PayPal)  
- Product categories and search filters  
- Inventory management and order tracking  
- Mobile-friendly shopping experience

### 5. Professional Feature Updates
- CMS Setup (WordPress or preferred platform)  
- Multimedia Embedding (YouTube, Vimeo, podcast feeds)  
- Online Giving Platform with recurring options  
- SEO Optimization and performance tuning  
- Accessibility Compliance (WCAG standards)

### 6. Testing & Launch
- Cross-browser and device testing  
- Soft launch for internal feedback  
- Public launch with announcement campaign  
- Training for staff on CMS and store management

### 7. Post-Launch Support
- Monthly backups and security checks  
- Quarterly UX and SEO reviews  
- Content updates and sermon uploads  
- Analytics monitoring and reporting

---

## 🎨 Design Variations

Three homepage layout concepts will be presented:

1. **Bold & Vibrant** – Inspired by Radiant Church  
2. **Minimal & Modern** – Inspired by Meta Church  
3. **Warm & Welcoming** – Hybrid approach emphasizing community
