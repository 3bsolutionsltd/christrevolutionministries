# Security Policy

## Reporting Security Issues

At Christ Revolution Ministries, we take security seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in any of our websites or services, we encourage you to report it to us promptly.

### How to Report a Security Issue

Please send security-related issues to:
- Email: info@christrevolutionministries.org
- Subject Line: [Security] - Brief description of the issue

### What to Include in Your Report

To help us better understand and address the issue, please include:

1. A description of the issue and its potential impact
2. Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
3. Any relevant screenshots or proof of concept
4. The affected URL(s)
5. Your contact information for follow-up questions

### Our Commitment

We commit to:
- Acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours
- Provide regular updates about our progress
- Not take legal action against security researchers who follow responsible disclosure practices
- Address the issue promptly based on its severity

### What to Expect

1. Acknowledgment within 24 hours
2. Initial assessment within 48 hours
3. Regular updates on our progress
4. Public recognition (if desired) after the issue is resolved

### Out of Scope

The following activities are prohibited:
- Testing on production systems that could impact service availability
- Social engineering attempts against our staff or congregation
- Physical attempts to access our facilities
- Automated vulnerability scanning without prior approval

### Safe Harbor

We support responsible security research and will not pursue legal action against individuals who:
- Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, data destruction, and service interruption
- Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to prove the vulnerability exists
- Provide us reasonable time to respond to vulnerability reports before making them public
- Do not violate any other applicable laws or regulations

Thank you for helping keep Christ Revolution Ministries secure!
